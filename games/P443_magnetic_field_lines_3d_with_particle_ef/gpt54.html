Magnetic Fields

Magnetic field lines show the direction a north test pole would move. Outside a magnet, field lines flow from the north pole toward the south pole.

In this model, particles move along idealized dipole-like field curves. The slider changes the effective field strength, which increases particle speed.

You can drag the poles to reshape the field, flip polarity, and add more moving charges to observe how motion changes.
