Solar Eclipse & Anatomical Reference

A solar eclipse occurs when the Moon passes between Earth and the Sun, thereby obscuring the view of the Sun from a small part of the Earth, totally or partially. The anatomical model represents the observer's nervous system response to this celestial event. Press 'e' to close.
